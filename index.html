My Story

I have been designing and developing websites for over 15 years now. My first job as a website designer came when I participated in the college internship required for my degree program. My advisor arranged this internship with a local non-profit organization named the Southeastern Missouri Area Health Education Center, or SEMO AHEC for short. I've re-designed their website twice since then.

After completion of my internship, I began a trend of working for non-profit organizations. My primary job title and role would generally be administrative in nature. However, I always ended up performing the duties of a web designer and developer along with continuing to do the work required for my primary role.

I think it is time for a change now.
